Demo: Dumbbell Wrist Curl

Dumbbell Wrist Curl

⭐ Flexor Carpi · Finger Flexors

Sit on a chair, rest forearm on thigh with palm up. Let the 20 lb dumbbell roll to fingertips, then curl the wrist up fully.

5×15 REPS

Demo: Reverse Wrist Curl

Reverse Wrist Curl

⭐ Extensor Group · Brachioradialis

Same position but palm facing DOWN. Curl the wrist upward. Crucial for wrist stability under pressure.

4×15 REPS

Demo: Static Grip

Dumbbell Hold Static Grip

Crushing Grip · All Finger Flexors

Hold the 30 lb dumbbell at the end of the handle (not the center) for maximum leverage challenge. Hold over a bucket.

5×45s EACH

🔵 Pronation / Supination Power

▶
Demo: Pronation Curl

Dumbbell Pronation Curl

⭐ Pronator Teres — #1 Arm Wrestling Muscle

Hold 20 lb dumbbell at one end (like a frying pan). Elbow at 90°. Rotate forearm so the weighted end goes DOWN and inward.

5×15 EACH
▶
Demo: Hammer Curl

Hammer Curl

⭐ Brachioradialis · Brachialis

Neutral grip (thumbs up). Curl the 30 lb dumbbell slow on the way down (3 seconds). The single best mass builder.

5×10 REPS
